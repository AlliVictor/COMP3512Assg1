About Us

This website is a COMP3512 assignment, which contains a database of hit songs in Spotify between 2016-2019. The user can navigate through the database and add their favorite song to their favorite list, as well as remove a song from their list. In addition, the user can view the information about a specific song that outlines the data of it.
