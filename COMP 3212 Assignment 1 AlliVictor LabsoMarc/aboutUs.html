About Us

This website is a COMP3512 assignment, which contains a database of hit songs
in Spotify between 2016-2019. The user can navigate through the database in the browse tab and add their favorite song to their favorite list, as well as remove a
song from their list. In addition, the user can view the information about a specific
song that outlines the data of it. Data of a song includes its BPM, energy, danceability, liveness, valence, acousticness, speechiness, and popularity. The song info page will display who made the song, what type of song it is, such as a solo song or
group, what type of genre it is, when it was created, and the duration of the song.
